# 🎬 ReactGuard – Demo Video Script & Showcase Guide

This script is designed for a **3-minute high-impact video**. It focuses on the "Magic Moment" of Somnia Reactivity.

---

## 📽️ The Script

### 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook (The Problem)
*   **Visual:** Show the Dashboard with the system in "ACTIVE" state. Point the cursor to the "Lending Pool" card.
*   **Voiceover:** "DeFi is broken. When an oracle is manipulated, protocols lose millions in seconds because traditional blockchains are passive. Off-chain bots are slow, expensive, and centralized. Meet **ReactGuard**—the first DeFi guardian built with **Somnia Native Reactivity**."

### 0:30 - 1:15 | The Solution (Dashboard Tour)
*   **Visual:** Hover over the "Reactivity Engine" card. Highlight the **On-Chain Subscription ID**.
*   **Voiceover:** "ReactGuard doesn't wait for a bot to tell it there's a problem. We've registered an on-chain subscription directly with the Somnia network. Our risk engine is a smart contract that lives on-chain and calculates weighted risk scores natively. No middleman, no delays."

### 1:15 - 2:00 | The Masterclass (The Attack)
*   **Visual:** Click **"Simulate Attack"**. Watch the logs populate. Show the top bar turning **RED**.
*   **Voiceover:** "Watch this. I'm triggering a 20% price drop. In a single atomic event, the Somnia network detects the change and invokes our ReactGuard contract. **Boom!** In less than 200ms, the lending pool is paused autonomously. Zero transactions were sent from our backend. This is pure on-chain reactivity."

### 2:00 - 2:45 | The Proof (Shannon Explorer Walkthrough)
*   **Visual:** Copy the Hash from the dashboard. Go to [Somnia Explorer](https://shannon-explorer.somnia.network/).
*   **Voiceover:** "Let's look at the raw data. I'm copying the hash of our attack. On the Shannon Explorer, you can see this transaction setting the price on our Oracle. But look deeper—click the **Internal Transactions** tab. You'll see a successful call from address **0x0100**. That is the Somnia network itself detecting the spike and immediately commanding our ReactGuard contract to defend. Then, look at the **Lending Pool** contract; you'll see it was paused in the exact same sequence. Pure, autonomous, on-chain truth."

### 2:40 - 3:00 | Call to Action (The Future)
*   **Visual:** Show the "System ACTIVE" state again after a reset. Show the logo.
*   **Voiceover:** "ReactGuard is the future of trustless DeFi security. Autonomous, sub-second, and 100% on-chain. Built for the Somnia ecosystem. Protect your protocol today."

## 💡 How to Showcase the Web Interface

### 1. Show the "Zero-Click" Badge
Make sure the camera/screen capture stays on the **Zero-Click Demo Mode Active** badge for a few seconds. Explain that this makes for a seamless demo where the backend signs the attack, so judges don't get distracted by MetaMask popups.

### 2. Live Latency Timer
When the defense triggers, point to the **Defense Latency** card. It usually shows a number like `150ms` or `240ms`. Say: *"Look at that latency. That’s faster than a human could even blink, and it happened entirely on-chain."*

### 3. Copy Hash & Paste
Use the **Copy Button (📋)** next to the attack transaction hash. Live in the video, paste it into the search bar of the **Shannon Explorer**. This shows the judges the system is transparent and verifiable.

## 🎙️ Recording Tips
*   **Crystal Clear Audio:** Use a good mic or a quiet room.
*   **Cursor Focus:** Use a cursor highlighter (like a yellow circle) if possible so judges can follow where you click.
*   **Zoom In:** When showing transaction hashes or contract addresses on the explorer, zoom your browser in (Ctrl + Plus) so the text is readable on mobile.
